Quiet Moon

A 42-day quality-improvement pilot: calmer NICU environments & more skin-to-skin, while oxygen targets remain exactly as per unit policy.

Scope

Environment

  • Track noise, light, alarm counts.
  • Introduce quiet hours & dimming.

Care Exposure

  • Daily skin-to-skin minutes (chart-based).
  • Family coaching checklists.

Measures

  • % time SpO₂ in unit range (policy-set).
  • Desaturation/brady events per 24h.
  • Noise/light levels, skin-to-skin minutes.

42-Day Cadence

  1. Weeks 1-2: Baseline observation.
  2. Weeks 3-4: Bundle on (quiet hours, alarm hygiene, coaching).
  3. Weeks 5-6: Sustain & summarize.

Guardrails

  • Unit oxygen targets unchanged.
  • De-identified, aggregate data only.
  • Pause if events worsen ≥3 days in a row.
